# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We expect contributors and maintainers to keep discussions respectful, direct, and technically grounded.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Be respectful in disagreement.
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Share context, evidence, and reproduction details.
- Accept feedback and course-correct quickly.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination.
- Trolling, baiting, or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly ignoring repository rules after maintainers ask for correction.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or block participants when behavior harms the project.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, open a private maintainer contact through the security/reporting path in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
